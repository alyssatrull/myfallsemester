Detective Fiction

Course Description

Analysis of classic and modern texts of detective fiction selected from a variety of historical periods and cultural traditions. Emphasis on the structural elements of detective fiction, on its various sub-genres, and on its employment by writers from different cultures, ethnicities, and genders.

Teacher Name

Bob Canter

Who Are They?

Robert Canter is a senior instructor in the Department of English. Canter has delivered numerous national conference presentations on teaching, literary theory, film and history, and is currently developing a reader and rhetoric on empathy in/as literature. His research interests include American Literature and 20th Century Literature.
