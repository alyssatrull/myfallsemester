Course Title

Developing Online Content

Course Description

This course explores the concepts and practices related to developing intelligent content in the field of technical communication. Students will learn how to create content that is adaptable, reusable, and able to be delivered across multiple platforms and formats. The course will cover the use of metadata, structured authoring, (component) content management systems, and the application of artificial intelligence in content development.

Teacher Name

Carlos Evia

Who Are They?
