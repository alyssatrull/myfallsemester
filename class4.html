Course Title

Developing Online Content

Course Description

This course explores the concepts and practices related to developing intelligent content in the field of technical communication. Students will learn how to create content that is adaptable, reusable, and able to be delivered across multiple platforms and formats. The course will cover the use of metadata, structured authoring, (component) content management systems, and the application of artificial intelligence in content development.

Teacher Name

Carlos Evia

Who Are They?

Carlos Evia is the Associate Dean for Transdisciplinary Initiatives, Chief Technology Officer, and a professor in the School of Communication located in the College of Liberal Arts and Human Sciences at Virginia Tech. His research interests include content operations and content strategy. He has published 4 books. He completed his undergrad in communications at el Instituto de Ciencias Sociales de Merida, Mexico, and received his MSC at Universidad LaSalle in Mexico City.
